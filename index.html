      Intra-day Liquidity Risk in Banking Simulation

      May 2018

      Through the use of LCG and the generation of random variables we can replicate the behaviour of banking transactions. The primary objective of this simulation is to determine the correct ammount of USD, EUR and GBP the banking system has to have to avoid incurring costs of default and maximizing the quantity of our cash in hand.
